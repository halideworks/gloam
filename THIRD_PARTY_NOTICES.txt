Gloam third-party notices
=========================

This file summarizes third-party components redistributed with Gloam release
artifacts. It is not a substitute for the full license texts shipped by those
projects.

ArgyllCMS
---------

Gloam Full / installer builds bundle ArgyllCMS command-line executables such as
spotread.exe and dispwin.exe for colorimeter measurement and gamma-ramp fallback.

Project: https://www.argyllcms.com/
Bundled version: ArgyllCMS 3.5.0
License: GNU Affero General Public License version 3
Source: https://www.argyllcms.com/Argyll_V3.5.0_src.zip

Gloam invokes ArgyllCMS executables as separate processes and does not link
against ArgyllCMS code. The ArgyllCMS binaries are redistributed unmodified.
When updating the bundled ArgyllCMS version, update this notice, the pinned
download hash in the source tree, and the packaged license/source reference.
